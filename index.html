Fly Dubai Joins Emirates Group Roblox as Official Subsidiary

Strategic agreement strengthens EGR with the addition of the Fly Dubai Division. · August 14, 2025 · From the Office of the Chairman Of The Board, Tyler Nicholas
Read the full announcement
Emirates Group Roblox is proud to officially announce that Fly Dubai has joined our organization as an official subsidiary under a new affiliation and subsidiary agreement. This partnership marks a significant step in strengthening our group’s operational capabilities and expanding our presence within the Roblox aviation community.

Under the terms of the agreement, Fly Dubai will join Emirates Group Roblox as our Fly Dubai Division, operating as a recognized subsidiary while continuing to function with its own distinct identity, brand, and internal operations. The division will remain under the leadership of Chairwoman Ash, ensuring continuity in management and preserving the culture and values that have made Fly Dubai a respected name in the community.

This collaboration is designed to enhance cooperation between our airlines, improve operational efficiency, and provide our members and passengers with more diverse and high-quality aviation roleplay experiences. By bringing together the resources and expertise of both organizations, we aim to set new standards in the Roblox aviation sector and deliver even greater value to our community.

We extend our warmest welcome to the Fly Dubai team and look forward to the exciting opportunities this partnership will bring. This marks the beginning of a new chapter for Emirates Group Roblox and Fly Dubai, united in our shared vision of innovation, professionalism, and excellence in Roblox aviation.
